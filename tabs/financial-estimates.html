Current figures for EBAN 2010 event show that for 2 to 3 night bulk (around 100 to 150) stays in a 5 star hotel is around 140-170 Euros per person per night. Similarly, for a luxury 4 star hotel stay will cost 90-120 Euros. This will be similar for ECSA. Besides of the conference hotel, the participants can also find many other hotels with very reasonable prices starting from 50 euros per night. Usually early reservation/planning will reduce the prices.
