TRAVELLING TO ISTANBUL
BY AIR
There are daily direct flights from all major cities of the world to Istanbul airports. The prices vary depending on the time of booking. We therefore strongly suggest our guests to book their flights long in advance due to Istanbul air traffic is very busy in September. Oasis Travel ticketing department will be more than happy to assist participants inquiring convinient flights to Istanbul with reasonable rates. Please submit all your flight inquiries to oasis@oasis.com.tr.
BY SEA
There are daily ferries from some Greek Islands and Black Sea ports. The cruise boats call Istanbul regularly until the end of October.
BY CAR
Guests coming from East Europe countries can enter Turkey from Greek & Bulgarian border gates of Turkey. There are regular train and coach lines from major European cities. Please check with Oasis Travel for bus and train tickets.
